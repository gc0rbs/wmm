# The Frontier — Marketing & Content Kit

> Ready-to-use copy and content starters. Public brand name is **The Frontier**
> (the codebase still uses "westward" internally — package name, DB name — which is
> fine to leave). Region names below use the **frontier (English) names** as
> player-facing, with formal names available as legacy/subtitle.
>
> **Honesty guardrails for all copy:**
> - **No story/lore campaign exists yet** — lead with settlement, economy,
>   collaboration, and combat (all real). Don't promise a narrative campaign.
> - **P2E is in development** — rewards are earned and tracked now, but no real
>   on-chain tokens move yet. "Earn for building" is fair; "withdraw crypto today"
>   is not.

## Short description (~280 chars)

> **The Frontier** is a browser MMO about taming a wild continent *together*. Drop
> into a vast shared world, build settlements from scratch, craft every tool and
> weapon, defend your claim in tactical battles, and trade in a 100% player-run

## Elevator pitch

> **The Frontier** is a collaborative MMORPG. Players land on an untamed continent
> and settle it from nothing — raising towns, crafting the entire tech tree from raw
> ore and timber, defending their claims from wolves, bears, and hostile natives in
> tactical turn-based battles, and trading in a fully player-driven economy. There
> are no solo heroes: four interdependent class paths and region-wide goals mean a
> settlement only grows because its community makes it grow. And now the work pays —
> contribute to your region's rise and earn rewards.

## The world: zone tiers (marketing-ready)

The Frontier is a **graded world** — the farther you push, the more it gives and takes.

| Tier | Vibe | Sample regions |
|------|------|----------------|
| 🟢 **Safe** | Civilized starting towns | Cedar Crossing, Red Mesa, Copper Gulch, Willow Bend |
| 🟠 **Frontier** | The contested edge | Broken Ridge, Dry Creek Pass, Rattlesnake Flats, Salt Wash |
| 🔴 **Remote** | Wild, rich, deadly | Ghost Canyon, Sulfur Bottom, Old Fort Bluff, Quartz Hollow |
| 🌊 **Sea** | The watery borders | Western Deep, Willow Bend Sea, Red Mesa Sea |

**Zone blurb:**
> The Frontier is a graded world. Start safe in **Cedar Crossing** or **Red Mesa**,
> where towns already stand. Push into the **frontier** — **Broken Ridge**,
> **Rattlesnake Flats**, **Dry Creek Pass** — where the land fights back. Brave the
> **remote** wilds of **Ghost Canyon** and **Sulfur Bottom** for the richest spoils.

## FAQ snippets

- **What is it?** A browser-based collaborative MMORPG — part survival, part
  town-builder, part player economy.
- **Solo or multiplayer?** Multiplayer to the core; the world is built around players
  helping each other.
- **What do I do?** Explore, gather, build, craft, trade, fight — and push region-wide
  goals forward with your community.
- **How big is the world?** A single shared continent of 23 regions, graded from safe
  towns to deadly remote wilds.
- **Combat?** Yes — tactical, turn-based, against wildlife and hostile natives.
- **The "earn" part?** You earn for hitting collaborative milestones. *(On-chain
  payout is in development.)*

## Content series ideas

- **"Frontier Field Guide"** — one explainer per system: the crafting tree, the four
  classes, how battles work, how the economy prices itself.
- **"Region Spotlight"** — feature a region by tier (Cedar Crossing → Broken Ridge →
  Ghost Canyon); the tier gives instant stakes. Template:
  > 📍 **Ghost Canyon** — *Remote*
  > Far from the safety of the settled towns, Ghost Canyon hides some of The
  > Frontier's richest veins… and its hungriest predators. Only the well-prepared
  > come back. Who's brave enough?
- **"Bestiary"** — a card per enemy (Gray/Black/White wolf, the Arth bear, Civ
  Warrior/Archer) with art + stats.
- **"From ore to sword"** — visualize the crafting chain
  (Wood→Coal→Carbon→Steel→Sword); highly shareable.
- **"World map reveal"** — the seas are named after their coasts (Willow Bend →
  Willow Bend Bay/Sea), so the map reads like a real place. Great reveal moment.
